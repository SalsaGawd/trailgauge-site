01
A gauge, not a map app

Most trail apps draw a line on a map. TrailGauge reads the terrain itself — the angles, the grade, the conditions — for people who actually leave the pavement.

TrailGauge is a mobile trail app for off-roaders, overlanders, and backcountry explorers. It combines a real-time pitch-and-roll inclinometer, grade-colored route logging, and live wildfire and weather alerts into one tool built for the trail.

Whether you're crawling a rocky climb or logging a dispersed-camping route, it keeps the data you care about in front of you — and lets you turn the trip into something physical you can hold and mail. Fire and weather alerts are always free, and never behind a paywall.

How it works

From the trail to a mailbox — or to the full instrument — in a few taps.

STEP 01

Run the trail

Open TrailGauge and drive. It logs your grade, angles, and route as you go.

STEP 02

Buy or subscribe

Order postcards as a one-time purchase, or subscribe to Pro for the full feature set — right in the app.

STEP 03

We print & mail

Your postcard is printed and mailed through our fulfillment partner, straight to whoever you choose.

How you're charged: Postcards are one-time purchases charged at checkout. TrailGauge Pro is a recurring subscription billed monthly or annually, and can be cancelled anytime.
